# Citation Style Rules

Comprehensive guide for APA, MLA, IEEE, and GB/T7714 citation styles.

---

## APA 7th Edition

### In-Text Citations

**Parenthetical:**
- Single author: (Smith, 2023)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2022)
- Three+ authors: (Smith et al., 2021)
- Organization: (World Health Organization [WHO], 2020)
- Direct quote: (Smith, 2023, p. 45)

**Narrative:**
- Smith (2023) found that...
- According to Smith and Jones (2022)...
- Research by Smith et al. (2021) indicates...

**Special Cases:**
- No author: Use short title ("Study Finds," 2023)
- No date: (n.d.)
- Multiple works same author: (Smith, 2020, 2021, 2022)
- Multiple works different authors: (Brown, 2020; Jones, 2021; Smith, 2022)

### Reference List Format

**Journal Article:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. Title of Periodical, volume(issue), page range. https://doi.org/xxxxx

**Book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle (Edition). Publisher.

**Book Chapter:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), Title of book (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.

**Website:**
Author, A. A., or Organization Name. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. URL

**Conference Paper:**
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of paper. In E. E. Editor (Eds.), Title of conference proceedings (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx

**Report:**
Organization Name. (Year). Title of report (Report No. xxx). Publisher. URL

## MLA 9th Edition

### In-Text Citations

**Basic format:**
- Single author: (Smith 45)
- Two authors: (Smith and Jones 45)
- Three+ authors: (Smith et al. 45)
- No page number: (Smith)

**Special Cases:**
- Author in text: According to Smith, "quote" (45).
- Title in text: "Article Title" states that... (Smith 45).
- Multiple works same author: (Smith, "Short Title" 45)
- Multiple works different authors: (Smith 45; Jones 32; Brown 12)

### Works Cited Format

**Journal Article:**
Author Last, First. "Article Title." Journal Name, vol. #, no. #, Year, pp. xx-xx. Database, DOI or URL.

**Book:**
Author Last, First. Book Title. Publisher, Year.

**Book Chapter:**
Author Last, First. "Chapter Title." Book Title, edited by Editor First Last, Publisher, Year, pp. xx-xx.

**Website:**
Author Last, First, or Organization. "Page Title." Website Name, Publisher (if different), Date, URL.

**Conference Paper:**
Author Last, First. "Paper Title." Conference Name, Date, Location. Database, URL.

**Report:**
Organization. Report Title. Publisher, Year, URL.

## IEEE

### In-Text Citations

**Format:**
- Single citation: [1]
- Multiple citations: [1], [2], [3] or [1]-[3]
- Multiple non-consecutive: [1], [3], [5]
- Author mentioned: Smith [1] found that...

**Placement:**
- Before punctuation: ...as shown [1].
- Within sentence: As shown in [1], the results...
- Multiple at once: ...demonstrated [1], [2], [3].

### Reference List Format

**Journal Article:**
[#] A. Author, "Article Title," Journal Name, vol. x, no. x, pp. xx-xx, Month Year.

**Book:**
[#] A. Author, Book Title, xth ed. City, State/Country: Publisher, Year.

**Book Chapter:**
[#] A. Author, "Chapter Title," in Book Title, A. Editor, Ed. City: Publisher, Year, pp. xx-xx.

**Website:**
[#] "Page Title," Site Name. URL (accessed Date).

**Conference Paper:**
[#] A. Author, "Paper Title," in Proc. Conference Name, City, Year, pp. xx-xx.

**Report:**
[#] Organization, "Report Title," Report Number, Month Year.

## GB/T7714 (Chinese Standard)

### In-Text Citations

**Sequential numbering:**
- Single citation: [1]
- Multiple citations: [1,2] or [1-3]
- Author mentioned: Smith等[1]发现...

**Placement:**
- Superscript (common): ...研究表明[1]。
- Brackets: ...研究表明[1]。

### Reference List Format

**Journal Article (Journal):**
[#] 作者. 题名[J]. 刊名, 年, 卷(期): 起止页码.
[#] Author. Title[J]. Journal Name, Year, Vol(Issue): Pages.

**Book (Monograph):**
[#] 作者. 书名[M]. 版次. 出版地: 出版者, 年: 起止页码.
[#] Author. Book Title[M]. Edition. City: Publisher, Year: Pages.

**Book Chapter:**
[#] 作者. 题名[M]//编者. 书名. 版次. 出版地: 出版者, 年: 起止页码.

**Thesis (Dissertation):**
[#] 作者. 题名[D]. 保存地: 保存单位, 年份.

**Conference Paper:**
[#] 作者. 题名[C]//编者. 论文集名. 出版地: 出版者, 年: 起止页码.

**Standard:**
[#] 标准制定者. 标准编号 标准名称[S]. 出版地: 出版者, 年.

**Electronic Resource:**
[#] 作者. 题名[EB/OL]. (发布日期)[引用日期]. URL.

**Report:**
[#] 作者. 题名[R]. 出版地: 出版者, 年.

**Patent:**
[#] 专利所有者. 专利名称: 专利号[P]. 年-月-日.

## Quick Comparison

| Element | APA | MLA | IEEE | GB/T7714 |
|---------|-----|-----|------|----------|
| In-text | (Author, Year) | (Author Page) | [Number] | [Number] |
| Author names | Last, F. M. | Last, First | F. M. Last | 作者 or Author |
| Year position | After author | End of entry | After pages | After journal |
| Title capitalization | Sentence case | Title Case | Title Case | As original |
| Article quotes | No | Yes | Yes | No |
| DOI/URL | Include | Include | Optional | Required for web |

## Common Mistakes

### APA
- ❌ (Smith, J., 2023)
- ✅ (Smith, 2023)
- ❌ & in narrative citation
- ✅ Smith and Jones (2023)

### MLA
- ❌ (Smith, p. 45)
- ✅ (Smith 45)
- ❌ Page number alone in narrative
- ✅ Smith writes that... (45).

### IEEE
- ❌ Smith [1] found.
- ✅ Smith [1] found (comma before citation)
- ❌ [1-3,5]
- ✅ [1]-[3], [5]

### GB/T7714
- ❌ 英文文献用中文标点
- ✅ 英文文献用英文标点
- ❌ 卷期格式: Vol.1 No.1
- ✅ 卷期格式: 1(1)

## Citation Management Tips

1. **Record as you go** - Don't wait until the end
2. **Be consistent** - Use one style throughout
3. **Check details** - Verify all information
4. **Use tools** - Consider citation managers
5. **Review carefully** - Check for errors before submission
